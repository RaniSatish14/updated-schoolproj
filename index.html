Welcome to
Vidya Degula School, Mysore
Vidya Degula School (VDS) is the flagship brand of the pioneering group of educational institutions head quartered in Bangalore, India. The VDS, Mysore commenced its academic activities in 2013 under the chairmanship of Dr K P Gopalkrishna. The school is located on a 5.5 acre campus, just 5 kms away from Infosys Campus, Mysore, 1 km from the ring road (between Hunsur Road junction and Bogadi junction).
